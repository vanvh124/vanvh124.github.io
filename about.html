V&L Machine is a privately held company in Garden Grove, CA and is a Single Location business. It was established in 1997 and incorporated in California.


In 1997, V&L Machine Incorporated started out as a small company with limited machinery and resources. Since then we have worked hard and built a solid foundation for our business, growing into a well-respected corporation in the industry.


For more than 20 years, we have excelled at establishing and maintaining strong relationships with our customers by manufacturing quality products and providing responsive, cooperative services.


We welcome the opportunity to earn your business through exceptional performance. The more complex your parts, the tighter your tolerances, the more you'll appreciate what V&L Machine Incorporated can do for you.
